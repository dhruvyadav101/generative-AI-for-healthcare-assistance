About Skin Diseases

Skin diseases affect millions of people worldwide. Early detection and proper diagnosis are key to effective treatment.

Our platform harnesses advanced machine learning models to analyze skin lesion images and provide a preliminary diagnosis. Please note that this tool is for informational purposes only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice.

Common Skin Diseases:

  • BCC (Basal Cell Carcinoma): The most common form of skin cancer.
  • BKL (Benign Keratosis-like Lesions): A group of benign skin growths.
  • MEL (Melanoma): A dangerous form of skin cancer.
  • NV (Nevus): Benign moles or skin lesions.
  • SCC (Squamous Cell Carcinoma): Another common type of skin cancer.

For more information about any skin condition, please consult a healthcare professional.
